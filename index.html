My Personal Top 5 Egg Dishes

Eggs Benedict

An American dish consisting two halves of an English muffin, each topped with Canadian bacon, a poached egg, and hollandaise sauce (an emulsion of egg yolk, melted butter, and lemon juice). I tried to make them during quaratine, and it turned out really good!

Tea Egg

A traditional Chinese savory food commonly sold as a snack, in which a boiled egg is cracked slightly and then boiled again in tea, sauce, and spice, creating this marble-like surface. This is one of my favorite dishes when I was a kid.

Egg Tofu

A Japanese steamed tofu made of eggs and dashi mixture. It can be cooked in soup, fried, steamed...etc. I love to eat them with instant noodles!

Quiche

A French tart consisting of pastry crust filled with savoury custard and eggs, and pieces of cheese, meat, seafood or vegetables. One of my favorite French cuisine.

Fried Egg (Sunny Side Up!)

A simple dish that is mostly seen in breakfast. I can't live my day without this in the morning.
